ℹ️ About IntelliExaChain
Our Mission

Our mission is to establish absolute trust, unwavering integrity, and complete accessibility for global examinations. By combining state-of-the-art AI proctoring with immutable cryptographic ledgers, we ensure that every score is earned fairly and verified instantly. IntelliExaChain is engineered to modernize high-stakes testing, rendering paper leaks and administrative manipulation impossible.

🛡️ Core Technology Pillars
🤖 Spectator AI

Client-side computer vision engine that monitors candidates via webcam. Tracks gaze vector deviations, secondary face intrusions, and anomalous ambient audio. Automatically logs incident hashes directly to the blockchain nodes.

⛓️ ExaChain Ledger

A permissioned, high-frequency blockchain network hosted across academic and government nodes. Stores cryptographic hashes of exam questions, response state snapshots, and proctoring audit trails, preventing unauthorized database tampering.

💳 Decentralized DIDs

Self-sovereign identity using W3C-compliant Decentralized Identifiers. Protects candidate privacy by keeping biometrics (FaceID/VoiceID templates) encrypted on-device while generating zero-knowledge proofs for exam-day verification.
